Currently, I am a visiting PhD student at Technion, where I work with [Alex Bronstein](https://bron.cs.technion.ac.il/). --- **What makes us humans?** I am interested in the intelligence that accounts for the difference existing between human beings and the other animals. To me this gap is on par to the one between life and non-life and is the consequence of a fundamental property of nature, that should be understood in terms of information processing. Currently, I am persuaded that this intelligence coincides with our use of symbols, and that we shall model the mechanism through which humans link meaning to new signs. As when a scientist proposes a new theory. **Highlights of my research in this direction** - When a ML system becomes an artificial scientist: mastering the game of [Zendo](https://www.nickbentley.games/zendo-as-a-tool-for-teaching-the-scientific-method/) with Transformers.
